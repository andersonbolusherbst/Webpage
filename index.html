Use Cases and FAQ

What Can HAB LABS do for my business?

Marketing

We support marketers and businesses in truly making data-driven decisions and uncovering insights. If you have data linked to your sales,such as conversions from Facebook Ad campaigns, why not learn the characteristics of your of your customers and which products/services they prefer?

Segment your customers so that you can boost your revenue and reduce costs and by allocating your resources more efficiently. Armed with the knowledge of who your customers are, you can divert your ad budget away from those who are unlikely to purchase your product or service towards your most loyal customers or "biggest spenders".

Operations

Is your business model subscription based? Predict your retention rates and monthly customer churn using machine learning classification algorithms. Use this info to manage your capacity and costs. Further, learn the characteristics of customers who are most likely to cancel and use this information to provide them with tailored content and offers to improve retention rates.

General & Automations

Do you use multiple platforms and software? We can create dashboards and web applications by connecting to API's to important metrics and data that your company wants to track, combining multiple sources of data into one readable, user-friendly location. These can be updated automatically depending on your needs and available data. We can set up automated alerts for you when certain thresholds or criteria are met.

Do you find yourself conducting the same analysis and reporting every month? Why not automate these? Do your employees need to log and capture data? Why not create a custom web app and database for your business?
